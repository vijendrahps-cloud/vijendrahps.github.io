Refund Policy

Last Updated: December 21, 2025

1. Refund Eligibility

Refunds are processed for eligible requests within 30 days of purchase. Digital products (PDF study materials) can be refunded if: (a) The content is significantly different from the description, (b) Technical issues prevent access, (c) Duplicate charges occur, (d) Unauthorized transactions are reported within 30 days.

2. Non-Refundable Items

The following are NON-REFUNDABLE: (a) Products accessed or downloaded, (b) Requests made after 30 days, (c) Requests without valid reason, (d) Products purchased from unauthorized resellers, (e) Services already rendered.

3. Refund Process

To request a refund: (1) Email support@studynotes-pro.in with your order ID, reason, and proof. (2) We will respond within 5 business days. (3) Upon approval, refunds are processed to the original payment method within 7-10 business days. (4) Bank processing may take additional 3-5 days.

4. Razorpay Refund Terms

All refunds are processed through Razorpay. Refund timelines depend on your bank's processing time. Razorpay Refund Policy applies. Contact support if refund is not received within 15 days.

5. Chargeback & Dispute Resolution

If you initiate a chargeback without contacting support first, StudyNotes Pro reserves the right to block your account. For disputes, contact us directly before involving payment processors.

6. GST & Tax Refunds

GST included in the purchase price will be refunded as per Indian tax regulations if the order is cancelled within 30 days.

7. Partial Refunds

Partial refunds are offered for: (a) Incomplete product delivery, (b) Partial access issues, (c) Payment errors. Full refunds are only available if no content has been accessed.
